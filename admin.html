Admission Procedure

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Meritorious Students: Students who have demonstrated academic excellence in their previous studies are eligible for admission. These students are rewarded with a 100% scholarship on tuition fees. This ensures that top performers can pursue their education without financial burden.
    Fee Structure: ₹0 (100% scholarship)
  • COVID Affected Students: We offer special consideration for students affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. These students are eligible for a 50% scholarship, acknowledging the challenges faced during these difficult times.
    Fee Structure: ₹50,000 (50% scholarship on a regular fee of ₹1,00,000)
  • Common Entrance Test: Admission through our Common Entrance Test (CET) is available for students who pass the examination. Successful candidates are offered a 75% scholarship on tuition fees, ensuring accessibility for deserving students.
    Fee Structure: ₹25,000 (75% scholarship on a regular fee of ₹1,00,000)
  • Management Quota: A limited number of seats are available under the management quota. These are for students who may not meet the other eligibility criteria but show potential in other areas. This quota does not come with a scholarship.
    Fee Structure: ₹1,00,000 (No scholarship)

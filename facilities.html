State-of-the-Art Facilities

Advanced Computer Labs

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software to support a variety of programming languages, design tools, and research projects.

Robotics Lab

The robotics lab offers hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology, allowing students to build and program robots for various applications.

Library and Resource Center

Our library is a hub of knowledge with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support student learning and research.

Innovation Hub

The Innovation Hub is a creative space where students can collaborate on projects, brainstorm ideas, and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Sports and Recreation

We believe in the holistic development of our students. Our sports facilities include a gymnasium, basketball court, and open fields for various outdoor activities.
